Frosted glass, elevated surfaces, premium restraint. The default aesthetic of modern SaaS.

✕Skip ifMobile performance is critical or content is text-heavy

Apple's keynote layout system, democratized. Asymmetric cards that make any feature feel flagship.

▶Best forLanding pages · Feature showcases · Marketing sites
◆EffortLow–Medium — CSS Grid is your entire toolkit
✕Skip ifYou need traditional navigation flows or dense informational content
